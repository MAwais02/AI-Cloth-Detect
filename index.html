About Fashion MNIST

Fashion MNIST is a dataset of Zalando's article images, consisting of 70,000 grayscale images in 10 categories. It serves as a direct drop-in replacement for the original MNIST dataset for benchmarking machine learning algorithms.

The 10 Classes:

  • T-shirt/Top
  • Trouser
  • Pullover
  • Dress
  • Coat
  • Sandal
  • Shirt
  • Sneaker
  • Bag
  • Ankle Boot

Each image is 28x28 pixels in grayscale. The model has been trained to recognize these specific categories with high accuracy. For best results, upload clear images with a single item centered in the frame.
